FOLO Ecommerce Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how FOLO ("we" or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our ecommerce application (the "Application").

Information We Collect

When you use our Application, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and shipping address.
  • Payment information, including credit card details and billing address, for processing your purchases.
  • Usage information, including your IP address, device information, and browsing activity within the Application.
  • Any other information you provide to us voluntarily through the Application.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Processing and fulfilling your orders and providing customer support.
  • Sending you order confirmations, shipping notifications, and other transaction-related communications.
  • Improving our Application and tailoring it to your preferences.
  • Sending you promotional offers, updates, and marketing communications if you have opted-in to receive them.
  • Preventing and detecting fraud, unauthorized access, or other illegal activities.

Information Sharing

We may share your information with third parties in the following circumstances:

  • With trusted service providers who assist us in operating our business, such as payment processors, shipping carriers, and marketing platforms.
  • In response to a legal request or when required by law to protect our rights, safety, or property.
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our business.
  • With your consent or at your direction.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, please note that no data transmission over the internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the revised policy will be effective immediately upon posting.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@foloecommerce.com.
